Community Outreach

Spreading our knowledge...

Mentoring DRC VEX IQ

One of the primary ways we support our Outreach program is through the mentoring of our VEX IQ students, and the basis we give them through our experience. We try and provide them with the resources and foundation they require in order to succeed once they reach the EDR stage. To do this, we not only help them understand their challenge objective, but we guide them step by step on how to reach there so they have a strong foundation of the Design Process, and a stronger understanding of Robotics as they advance stages. We also help with instructional basis, and through teaching them documentation, where they record their design process in the engineering notebook.

Hosting Competitions

The Dublin Robotics Club is the host of multiple VEX EDR AND VEX IQ tournaments throughout the course of a calendar year, where teams from throughout the bay area, mostly, travel to Dublin in order to participate. This not only gives us the chance to meet people and mingle, but a chance to build a strong platform socially and for the future of our club. We often feature in the newspaper of many schools, showing the excellence of our participants.  

Worldwide foundation

Each year, some of our students travel places worldwide in order to spread their knowledge and passion for robotics. Our club not only wants to be known for our success in competitions, but we also want the less fortunate to lay their hands on what we do, and take up the same interests, so that one day they have the same oppurtunity we did in order to take up a course like Mechanical Engineering, or computer sciences.

This year, Arya, from 3303D, traveled to India in order to spread the passion of robotics. He took them through the design process, and talked about some of the implications, background, end experience you might need to be successful in this major. This works as a major inspiration for the rest of us, as not only is he laying a foundation for our club, but he is providing the basis of success for the less fortunate.

Donations

Although this is still a work in progress, we are trying to donate to the shelterless, and the homeless in order to provide them with a new oppurtunity. This can be food, money, or anything they are in immediate need of. Although this is not in relations with robotics, we are trying to increase our social range, and by doing so we implement the idea of charity, and helping out the less unfortunate. This may not relate to our primary purpose, but being good citizens and somaritans are vital in order to strengthen our morales. In addition to helping the less unfortunate, we are also going to donate food, clothes, and money to Refugee families, so that we can pay back the people that once served our country, and are constantly making this world a better place.
